Dr. Thomas Wilson — Tutor and Editor

I help students complete assignments, on time and with immaculate attention to detail. I also assist job seekers to write effective selection criteria to help them get an interview.

BACKGROUND:

I have a Bachelor of Arts from the Australian National University, and a PhD in English from the University of Western Australia. I completed three years of a Bachelor of Science (Physiotherapy) at Notre Dame University, ranking in the top 10% of students. I hold a Graduate Diploma in Information and Library Studies from Curtin University. I now work at three different libraries in Perth, helping students and the public improve their information literacy. I previously worked as a Tutor in the Department of English and Cultural Studies at UWA, and am currently an Honorary Research Fellow at UWA. I am available by appointment at a meeting room at UWA. For $30 I will spend half an hour with you helping you to improve the quality of your assignment, essay, or job application. I am very effective with my use of time, and you will see big improvements in the quality of your work.
